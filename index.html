About Me

I am Amy! My artwork is meant to be profound and visually appealing. I engage in different cultural aspects to diverse my content. I take into consideration my different experiences and noticeable environment aspects in an attempt to experiment art-styles. I have used an art process of transferring real-world image into a digital mainframe. My is improvisational and arranged for the feelings at the moment. My muse comes from surroundeings and reoccuring issues going on to my life.

I am a first-generation student majoring in Speculative Design at University of California, San Diego. My expected graduation date is June 2023. I am working towards a Bachelor of Arts in Speculative Design (Emphasis: Computing/Design) with grade point average of 3.133. I attended South Gate High School from 08/2015-06/2019 and graduated June 5th, 2019 with grade point average of 3.680 GPA.

As an artist, I like to focus on digital art as for it is flexible for my needs. I create visualizations with layered concepts of space and proportion through Adobe Creative Cloud programs including Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, Dreamweaver and Adobe After Effects. I am fluent and bilingual in Spanish and English. I am certified in Adult and Pediatric First Aid/CPR/AED - valid until June 2020. I am proficient in Microsoft Office suite, including Word, PowerPoint, and Excel. I have beginner coding skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Bootstrap.
